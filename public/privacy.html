Neon Sentinel Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Neon Sentinel does not use advertising, third-party analytics, cross-site tracking, or data brokers.

What The Game Collects

Guest play can be used without signing in. When you submit or sign a score, the game may transmit the following data for game functionality:

  • Public key, used as a public user identifier for signed score claims.
  • Gameplay score data, including score, wave, rescues, run timing, and run metrics needed to validate leaderboard entries.
  • Network requests needed to publish or read public leaderboard data and to load game assets.

Public Relay Data

Signed leaderboard entries are designed to be public. They may be published to public Nostr relays and can be copied, stored, or displayed by relay operators and third-party Nostr clients outside Neon Sentinel's control.

Local Storage

The game stores gameplay preferences, display settings, cached leaderboard data, and cached relay/profile information on your device. You can remove this local data by clearing site data in your browser.

Data Retention And Deletion

Local data stays on your device until you clear site data. Neon Sentinel keeps server-side claim logs only as needed to validate, secure, and debug signed leaderboard functionality.

Signed leaderboard entries published to public Nostr relays may be retained by independent relay operators outside Neon Sentinel's control. If you want deletion help for data controlled by Neon Sentinel, email support@neonsentinel.com with your Nostr public key or score details.

Revoking Access

Guest play does not require an account. In web builds that expose optional Nostr signing, you can stop using it by signing out in the game or revoking/removing the key in your Nostr signer.

Payments And Tracking

Lightning, Geyser, and Ko-fi links are optional value-for-value support links. They are not required for gameplay, do not affect score, and are handled by their respective services. Neon Sentinel does not track you across sites or sell personal data.

Support

For support, use the support page at neonsentinel.com/support.html.
