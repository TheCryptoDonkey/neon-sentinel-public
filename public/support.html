Gameplay And Accounts

  • Guest mode is fully playable without signing in.
  • Optional Nostr login is used for signed public leaderboard entries and contact-based roster play.
  • Scores, credits, and leaderboard entries have no cash value and cannot be redeemed.
  • Value-for-value support links are optional and separate from gameplay score.

Data Requests

Email support@neonsentinel.com for privacy or deletion requests. Include your Nostr public key or score details if the request concerns signed leaderboard data.
